Welcome to HexMapGen!

Please insert values below, or leave them as default, and a map will be generated using them.

Forests are green, rivers are blue, roads are orange, and buildings are red.

  • Forests can spawn anywhere, and are more likely to spawn next to other forests

  • Rivers spawn from an edge, and meander across the map afterwards. Rivers can overwrite forests that are in their path.

  • Roads spawn from an edge and follow straight lines to another edge. Roads can overwrite rivers and forests in their path.

  • Buildings spawn next to roads or other buildings, and can overwrite forests, but not rivers or roads.
